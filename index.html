How to take the typing test

This test measures your typing speed and accuracy. Follow these steps:

  1. Select a difficulty from the dropdown (Easy / Medium / Hard).
  2. Start typing into the text area. The test automatically starts on your first keystroke.
  3. Live feedback: Each word in the sample turns blue when typed correctly and red when incorrect.
  4. Finish the test by pressing the Enter key. This will stop the timer and display your results.
  5. Results displayed: Level, Time (seconds, rounded to two decimals), and WPM (rounded to a whole number).
  6. Retry: Click the "Retry" button to load a new sample at the same difficulty. Retry clears the input, enables typing, resets results, and disables the Retry button until the next completion.

Accessibility & tips:

  • The test area is keyboard-focused when you begin typing.
  • Pressing Enter prevents a newline and ends the test to produce your final score.
  • If you change difficulty, a new sample is loaded and results are reset.
